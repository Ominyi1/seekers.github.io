VISION:

The kingdom seekers is a faith-base Christian fellowship that is aimed at drawing young christians and new converts to the foot of the cross. It is an interdenominational fellowship that teaches Bible principles and Christ and its crucificaxion.




The word of God is powerful. It is the power of God for changing men and it's the life and light of men. The heavens rejoice for a saved soul. At kingdom Seekers, we rejoice greatly to see perishing souls save for Christ.




FOLLOWSHIP IN SPIRIT AND IN TRUTH.

This fellowship is an avenue to purge our hearts to God while we are young and strong. This great fellowship has spiritual fathers and deaconess that have laid their all at the foot of the cross to teach and mentor youths for christ in this End-Time. This is a great move of God and we ought to aveil ourselves for the master use.
